Myriad — Creative Arts Journal

Founded in 1962, Myriad has been a well-respected outlet for the creative and diverse talent found on the El Camino College campus. Over the years it has showcased poetry and prose written by all community members within the college. For over 50 years, Myriad has been a student-run publication combining the talents of the English department with the vision and talent of the Arts department at El Camino College.

A collaborative publication.
